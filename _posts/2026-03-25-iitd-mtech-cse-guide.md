# IIT Delhi MTech in Computer Science and Engineering Guide

Welcome to the ultimate guide for pursuing your MTech in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Delhi. This guide will cover everything you need to know, from admission requirements to career prospects.

## Admission Requirements
- **Undergraduate Degree:** A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
- **Entrance Exam:** Candidates must qualify for the GATE exam.
- **Interviews:** Some candidates may be called for interviews based on their GATE scores.

## Course Structure
- **Duration:** 2 years (4 semesters)
- **Core Courses:** Data Structures, Algorithms, Software Engineering, etc.
- **Electives:** Choose your electives based on interests and career goals.

## Career Prospects
Graduates can pursue careers in:
- Software Development
- Research and Development
- Academia
- Data Science

## Conclusion
This guide serves as a starting point for aspiring MTech students at IIT Delhi. Make sure to check the official website for the most accurate and updated information.
